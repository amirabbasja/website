Research Experiences

University of Tehran

At the master’s level, research focused on the development of a device to help people with hand tremors. The work included nonlinear and recursive modeling of various dynamical systems, accurate biomechanical modeling of human hand motion, and custom numerical solvers implemented in MATLAB and Python. Optimal design parameters were obtained through large-scale numerical optimization leveraging parallel computation on high-performance computing systems. The device’s performance was evaluated using simulations driven by real-world tremor time-series data collected from patients. Post-master’s research centers on the application of advanced machine learning and reinforcement learning methods, incorporating and spiking neural networks, to biomechanical and engineering systems.
